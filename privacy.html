Privacy Policy Shipmate ("the App") is operated by eTechZim Private Limited. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our parcel delivery and courier platform. Information We Collect Account information: name, phone number, email address. Location data: pickup and delivery addresses, and real-time location while a delivery is in progress, to enable tracking and matching with couriers. Payment information: transaction details processed via EcoCash or other supported payment providers. We do not store your full payment credentials. Delivery details: parcel descriptions, recipient contact details, and delivery instructions you provide. Device information: device type, operating system, and app usage data for performance and security purposes. How We Use Your Information To facilitate parcel pickup, delivery, and courier matching. To process payments and maintain transaction records. To communicate with you about your deliveries, including notifications and support. To improve app performance, security, and user experience. Sharing of Information We share necessary delivery information (such as pickup/drop-off location and contact details) with the courier assigned to your delivery. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data Retention We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Your Rights You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us using the details below. Security We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
