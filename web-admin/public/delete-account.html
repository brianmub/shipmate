Account and Data Deletion Request

At ShipMate, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete via the ShipMate App (Recommended)

You can instantly delete your account and data directly within the mobile app:

  1. Open the ShipMate App and log in.
  2. Go to the Settings or Profile tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice. Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers immediately.

Option 2: Submit a Deletion Request via Email

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot log in, you can request data deletion by contacting our support team.

Email: support@shipmate.co.zw

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Body: Please include the phone number or email address associated with your account so we can verify and process your request.

Note: For security reasons, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing an email deletion request. Email requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

What happens when your account is deleted?

  • Your personal profile information (name, phone number, email) will be permanently erased.
  • Your active sessions will be terminated.
  • Your order history will be anonymized for financial and legal compliance purposes, meaning it can no longer be linked back to you.
