# Release Notes - Initial Release (1.0.0)

Welcome to the first release of ShipMate!

In this initial version, we are introducing our core features to connect customers with drivers for seamless logistics and delivery services.

Key Features in this release:
- **Customer App**: Easily request deliveries, track your shipments in real-time, and manage your delivery history.
- **Driver App**: Accept delivery requests, navigate to pickup and drop-off locations, and track your earnings.
- **Secure Authentication**: Secure login and registration for all users.
- **Real-time Updates**: Get live status updates on your orders.

Thank you for choosing ShipMate. We are continuously working to improve your experience. If you encounter any issues or have feedback, please reach out to our support team.
